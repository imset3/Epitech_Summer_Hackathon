TITLE: Sea surface temperatures keep rising, partly due to the appearance of a new El Niño
URL: https://www.jeuxvideo.com/news/2092472/la-temperature-de-la-mer-continue-d-augmenter-et-les-experts-sont-formels-ceci-est-du-en-partie-a-l-apparition-d-un-nouveau-phenomene-el-nino.htm

JVTech reports that sea surface temperatures continue to rise and that recent satellite data showed exceptionally high values around 21 June 2026. It says two major observation systems reached similar conclusions, strengthening the finding that ocean surface temperatures were at record levels for that time of year rather than reflecting a measurement error.

The article gives two key explanations for the anomaly: long-term industrial-era global warming and the emergence of a new El Niño episode. It notes that the World Meteorological Organization had already warned at the end of May that El Niño was likely, and that the U.S. NOAA Climate Prediction Center declared on 11 June 2026 that El Niño conditions were established in the equatorial Pacific.

JVTech emphasizes that El Niño alone does not explain all ocean warming, especially outside the tropical Pacific. The story describes a dangerous combination in which the natural El Niño cycle adds to the higher baseline created by human-driven climate change. It warns that warmer oceans evaporate more water and can provide more energy for severe storms, hurricanes and less predictable extreme weather.
