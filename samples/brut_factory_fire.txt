SOURCE: Brut
TITLE: Huge smoke cloud after factory fire near Lyon
DATE: 2026-07-08

Videos shared from Lyon show a large smoke cloud after a fire at a chemical factory. Brut spoke to residents who described a strong smell and said police blocked several roads. Some local witnesses claimed two people died and thirteen others were injured, but officials had not confirmed that figure at the time of publication. Emergency services said the situation was stabilising.
